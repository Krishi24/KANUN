Frequently Asked Questions

How do I sign up for a newsletter?

To sign up for a newsletter, click on the "Newsletter" tab in the top menu and enter your email address in the form provided. You will then receive email updates with the latest news from our channel.

How do I submit a news tip?

To submit a news tip, click on the "Contact" tab in the top menu and fill out the form provided. Please be sure to include as many details as possible so that our team can follow up on your tip.

How do I change my email preferences?

To change your email preferences, click on the "Settings" tab in the top menu and select the "Email Preferences" option. From there, you can adjust which newsletters you would like to receive and how often you would like to receive them.
