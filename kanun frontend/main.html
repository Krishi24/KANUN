Civil Laws

Civil Law comprises of set of rules and regulations which helps in resolving disputes which are non-criminal in nature. They include laws that are concerned with the private affairs of citizens. For example, marriage and property ownership.



Property Laws

Property law is the sum of rights and duties, privileges and no-rights, powers and liabilities, disabilities and immunities that exist with respect to things like legally protected claims to resources, such as land and personal property as well as intellectual property.



Citizenship laws

Citizenship is a legal status that means a person has a right to live in a state and that state cannot refuse them entry or deport them. This legal status may be conferred at birth, or, in some states, obtained through 'naturalisation'.
